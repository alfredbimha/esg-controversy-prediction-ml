Understanding ML for ESG Controversy Prediction

Can machine learning predict which companies will face ESG scandals?

The ML Pipeline

1Features: ESG, size, leverage, sector
2Train 3 models
3Evaluate on held-out test set
4Interpret with SHAP

Three Models Compared

Logistic Regression

Simple, interpretable. Each feature gets a coefficient you can explain. AUC ~0.74

Best performer

Random Forest

Ensemble of decision trees. Captures non-linear patterns. AUC ~0.66

Good baseline

Gradient Boosting

Sequentially builds trees that correct previous errors. AUC ~0.66

Complex model
SHAP analysis reveals that low ESG scores, high leverage, and certain sectors are the strongest predictors of controversy — giving investors early warning signals.
